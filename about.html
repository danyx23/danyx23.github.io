Hi! I’m Daniel.

I’m a software engineer and a photographer. I have used a wide variety of programming languages, from C++ to Python, R and C# and am now most interested in functional programming, especiall Elm, Haskell and F#. At the moment I work for a swiss company in the bioinformatics sector and on the side I develop an online photo slideshow editor using Elm and a serverless backend running on AWS Lambda.

My work as a photographer is online at Iconoclash Photography, the studio I run with my wife. I studied film directing and a couple of the films I made are still online at vimeo. I sometimes write about software engineering here on my blog and about politics, science and economics on ichahnezusammenhaenge.de. For random curiousities I use soup.io
