Hi! I’m Daniel.

I’m a software engineer and photographer based in Berlin. I currently work at Edelweiss Connect developing data solutions for scientists and I run the photography studio Iconoclash Photography together with my wonderful wife. I really like programming languages from the ML family (F#, Elm, Haskell, ...) and rant a lot about the state of software development today.

I studied film directing and a couple of the films I made are still online at vimeo. I sometimes write about software engineering here on my blog and about politics, science and economics on ichahnezusammenhaenge.de. For random curiousities I used soup.io though less and less in recent times.
